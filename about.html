About Sozo

We've sat across from hundreds of Florida families
on the hardest weeks of their year.

Sozo Behavioral Health Services is a team of licensed psychologists and clinicians who specialize in one thing: delivering complete, school-ready evaluations for kids, teens, and young adults — fast, accurate, and with the warmth your family deserves.

We don't make you drive across the state. We don't make you wait months. We don't hand you a long report and disappear. We give you clear answers you can use — Monday morning, with your child's teacher, in your own words.

When you call Sozo, you're not getting a phone tree and a waitlist. You're getting a person whose entire job is to help your family find the right answer, fast.

Our Promise

If we're not the right fit, we'll tell you — honestly.

After your free 15-minute call, if Sozo isn't the right fit for your family, we'll connect you with a clinician or program who is. At no cost. No pressure. No wasted time.

Helping the family in front of us — even when "helping" means pointing somewhere else — is what makes families recommend us to other families. That's how we've built Sozo, and it's how we plan to keep building it.

A child shouldn't be punished or left behind for how their brain actually works. They deserve to be understood — not put on a waiting list.

Ready when you are.
