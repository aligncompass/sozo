FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Do you take my insurance? +

We partner with new insurers regularly. Call us at 1-888-985-6037 and we'll verify your benefits in minutes.

How fast can we start? +

Most families start within 1–2 weeks. No waitlist. Appointments available 7 days a week, including evenings and weekends.

How many sessions does it take? +

Most evaluations are complete in 1–3 sessions, including the feedback session where we walk you through results and recommendations.

Will my child's school accept the report? +

Yes. Our reports are written in the exact language IEP and 504 teams expect, with accommodation recommendations spelled out clearly.

Telehealth — really, for kids? +

Yes, with validated protocols. On the intake call we'll recommend the best format for your child — and offer in-person if telehealth isn't a fit.

What does it cost? +

It depends on the evaluation type and your insurance. Most insured families pay $0 to a small copay. Self-pay rates and payment plans are available — call us and we'll walk you through the exact numbers for your situation.

Do you accept Step Up for Students? +

Yes — we are an approved Step Up for Students provider. Scholarship-funded evaluations are welcome.
